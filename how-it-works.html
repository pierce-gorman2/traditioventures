The Process

Steps To Partnership

I

Application

You tell us what you are building, where you are now, and why you believe God has called you to build it.

II

Initial Review

We review the man, the church connection, the calling, and whether the opportunity merits a deeper business conversation.

III

Business Evaluation

We examine market demand, business model, execution readiness, and whether the company can support durable long-term growth.

IV

Conversation & Partnership

If there is alignment, we move into direct conversation around structure, strategy, expectations, and what partnership should look like.

Criteria

What We Evaluate

These are the core areas we use to evaluate fit. Clean, simple, and taken seriously.

Church

Active membership in a local church is our first non-negotiable.

Calling

You need to clearly articulate why God has called you to build this.

Business Viability

The company has to work in the real world, not just in theory.

Coachability

We look for humility, maturity, and a willingness to be sharpened.

Generational Vision

We care whether this is being built to last and be passed down.

Partnership Fit

We want men who are ready for real accountability and real collaboration.
